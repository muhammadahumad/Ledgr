How Opening Balances Work in LEDGR

Opening balances are a single journal entry that sets your starting financial position. They represent your balance sheet on a specific date — typically:

  • The last day of your previous financial year (year-end close)
  • The date you switched from your old accounting software to LEDGR
Important: Debits must always equal Credits. If your trial balance from your old system has a balancing retained earnings figure, include it in account 3000 (Owner Equity) or 3100 (Retained Earnings).

What to include:

  • Assets (Cash, Bank, AR, Inventory) — enter as Debit
  • Liabilities (AP, Loans, Tax payable) — enter as Credit
  • Equity (Owner equity, Retained earnings) — enter as Credit
  • Leave Revenue and Expense accounts at zero (they start fresh each year)

Getting your trial balance from:

  • QuickBooks: Reports → Trial Balance → Export to Excel
  • Xero: Accounting → Reports → Trial Balance → Export CSV
  • Tally: Gateway → Display → Trial Balance → Export
  • Excel/Manual: Use the CSV Import tab with Account Code, Name, Debit, Credit columns
