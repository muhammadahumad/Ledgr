📖 Migration Guide — Step by Step

Step 1 — Opening Balances
Go to Opening Balances. Enter your balance sheet as of Dec 31, 2024 (or start of your migration date). This sets cash, AR, AP, loans, equity.

Step 2 — Import Sales Invoices
Export invoices from your old system. In QuickBooks: Reports → Sales → Invoice List → Export CSV. In Xero: Accounts → Sales → Export. Paste here → Import.

Step 3 — Import Supplier Bills
Export bills from your old system. In QuickBooks: Reports → Expenses → Vendor Balance Detail → Export. Paste here → Import.

Step 4 — Verify
Check the P&L report and Balance Sheet. The numbers should match your old system's reports for the same period.

Step 5 — Year-End Close
Once all 2025 transactions are imported and verified, run the Year-End Close. This finalises 2025 and prepares LEDGR for 2026.

💡 For Akira & Co: If transactions exist from Jan 2025 onwards and year-end was not done, skip Step 1 (opening balance) and go straight to Steps 2 & 3 to import all 2025 transactions. Then run Year-End Close on Dec 31, 2025.
