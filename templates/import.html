📋 Migrating from QuickBooks Online / Xero / Odoo?
Recommended approach — Clean Start as of a cut-off date:
1. Export your Chart of Accounts, Customers, and Suppliers as CSV from your old system
2. Import them below using the import tools
3. Enter Opening Balances (Total assets, liabilities, equity) as of your cut-off date via Journal → Manual Entry
4. Start recording new transactions in LEDGR from your cut-off date forward
5. Keep your old system read-only for historical reference

This is the standard method used by Xero, Odoo and all major accounting firms. Historical transaction migration is not recommended — it adds months of work and creates audit complications.
👥 Import Customers
QB: Reports → Customer Contact List → Export to CSV
Xero: Contacts → Filter Customers → Export
Required columns: Name, Email (optional), Phone (optional), Balance (optional)
↑
Drop CSV or click to upload
🏪 Import Suppliers / Vendors
QB: Reports → Vendor Contact List → Export to CSV
Xero: Contacts → Filter Suppliers → Export
Required columns: Name, Email (optional), Phone (optional)
↑
Drop CSV or click to upload

After importing contacts, enter your opening balances as of your migration date. This creates the starting Trial Balance.
